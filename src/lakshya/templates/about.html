About Lakshya

Lakshya bridges the alumni-insitute connect through an active and dedicated team. Our aims and objectives have matured over time to enable us to have a deeper and sustainable impact.

Innovation Project

Create ecosystem of research, innovation and entrepreneurship in NIT Warangal

Scholarship Project

Provide financial assistance to students in need of such aid

Asthra

Improve efficiency, transparency and accountability of the administration

Mission

Our mission is to enable NIT Warangal to efficiently develop an ecosystem of research and innovation while also making it accessible to all students.

Core Values

These are our guiding principles. Everytime we have a difficult decision to make, we come here.

  • If we have nothing to hide, we have nothing to fear
    We want your support to nurture our work, while also seeing where exactly your contributions are being utilized. Being transparent, both in funds and activities, keeps us on the right track.

  • Everyone has something to contribute
    While the alumni contribute immensely with their experience, the system at NIT Warangal is built around students and faculty and is run by the administration. Coming together is not a way, but the only way to form a holistic sustainable approach to achieve our shared vision of a better NIT Warangal.

  • There is a lot that technology can offer
    At the heart of our effort is making a point - technology can solve problems, create wealth and make life easier. Institutions like NIT Warangal need to breed technology, become hotbeds for cutting edge research and innovation. We do not use technology to assist us, technolgy forms the core of our work.
