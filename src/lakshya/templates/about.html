Core Values

Before we began our work, we prepared a list of values to guide us. Everytime we have a difficult decision to make, we visit this list.

  • Transparency
    We want your support to nurture our work, while also seeing where exactly your contributions are being utilized. Being transparent, both in funds and activities, keeps us on the right track - if we have nothing to hide, we have nothing to fear.

  • Sustainability
    We are in this for the long-haul. We work to have a lasting impact that will take us closer to our vision.
