About Asthra

Vision

To make the present Indian education system free of corruption through Citizen Action and a role model for transparency and accountability.

Mission

To make use of Right to Information (RTI) extensively to make all Institutes of National Importance (INI) transparent and accountable. To expand the same model to all educational institutes.

Our Work and impact

  • 14 RTI applications filed
  • Institute terminated over-priced web development contract in favour of in-house development by students
  • Reduction in tuition fees to the tune of Rs.3,500 was made possible through the ratification of the NITW fee system after Asthra RTI application revelead discrepency.
