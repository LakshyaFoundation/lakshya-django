Research Facilitator Program

This program provides funding to NIT Warangal UG and PG students whose work is chosen for presentation in top national and international conferences.

Selection Criteria

NIT Warangal UG and PG students are eligible. Students must receive selection confirmation from conference organizers.

Selection Process

  • Student mails selection confirmation and conference details
  • Lakshya committee evaluates conference ranking
  • Funding is provided based on conference ranking and after student confirms participation

Funding Criteria

Full funding (100% airfare + registration) for students selected for   Tier-1 conferences

Apply For Funding

To apply for funding, Please mail to info@thelakshyafoundation.org
