Innovation Project

The Innovation Challenge is part of the larger goal to build an atmosphere of research, innovation and entrepreneurship.

The aims of the Innovation Challenge are:

  • Encourage students and faculty to innovate
  • Increase interaction between institute and industry
  • Expose students to the idea of technology entrepreneurship
