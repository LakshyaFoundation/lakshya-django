Innovation Project

The Innovation Project is part of the larger goal to build an atmosphere of research, innovation and entrepreneurship in NIT Warangal.

The Innovation Project involves:

  • Encourage students and faculty to innovate
  • Increase interaction between institute and industry
  • Expose students to the idea of technology entrepreneurship
