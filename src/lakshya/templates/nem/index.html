Anand Sudarshan is an advisor & consultant, and is Founder & Director of Sylvant Advisors Private Limited. Anand is also an independent director at Manipal Global Education Services Private Limited (MaGE). Till June 2012, he was Vice-Chairman & Managing Director of MaGE.

 

He currently advises companies & entities in the education sector and is also a fund advisor with TVS Capital Funds. He is active in supporting Government of India in policy formulation, and is a member of select Committees & Task Forces of the Planning Commission of India as well as the Ministry of Human Resource Development.

 

Prior to his stint at MaGE, he was President, Adea International. In 1989 Mr. Sudarshan co-founded The Microland Group.Anand has a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering from NIT, Trichy and a PGDM from IIM Calcutta.

Chintalapati Srinivasa Raju, popularly known as Srini Raju, is an Indian Information Technology (IT) Professional, Entrepreneur, and Private Equity Investor. K Sharat Chandra is co-founder of Butterfly Fields which provides low-cost models for application-oriented learning to children from 8 to 16 years of age. Started in 2005, it works with government and private schools, and has developed models that integrate with the school curriculum, regardless of the board the school is affiliated to.

 

The company was self-funded in the initial years and worked on a bootstrap budget till in September 2010. In 2010, Butterfly Fields raised Rs.5 crore from Aavishkar, a social venture capital fund. Today the company has a reach of 5,00,000 children across over 3500 schools.

Prof. T. Srinivasa Rao is one of the senior Professors of the NIT-Tiruchirappalli with 25 years of experience in teaching, research and administration. He obtained his B.Tech.
