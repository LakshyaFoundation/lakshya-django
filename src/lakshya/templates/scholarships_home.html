Scholarships

Lakshya provides scholarships to students who cannot afford the fee.
Ours is a stress free loan model that is

  • interest free
  • collateral free
  • with repayment period of five years

We do a thorough verification (which includes going to the applicants house and verifing) so as to ensure the scholarships are given only to deserving students.
