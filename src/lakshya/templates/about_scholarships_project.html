About Financial Assistance Program

What is a Lakshya Scholarship?

Lakshya provides loans to students who cannot afford to pay their undergraduate fee. Important points to note here are:

  • The loan is interest free and no collateral is required.
  • Each scholar gets five years to repay the loan. Time spent in higher education is exempt.
  • The scholarship will cover tuition and hostel fees.

How do you ensure repayment?

Once a scholar graduates, they deposit a minimum of Rs.100 per month in the Lakshya account. This enables to keep in touch with each scholar and ensures that their contact information is up to date. We have had a 100% repayment record so far, and scholars have expressed their desire to not only repay the amount but also willingly contributed over and above it.

Who is eligible for a Lakshya Scholarship?

NIT Warangal undergraduate students are eligible for the scholarship. The criteria for selecting scholars are:

  • Need based - A student unable to pay for his/her tuition and hostel expenses will be considered.
  • Merit - If two students with similar needs are tied for the last available slot, the student with a better academic record will be chosen.

What is the process followed for choosing a scholar?

  • Lakshya calls for applications in the first semester every year, asking candidates to fill an online application form.
  • A shortlist is created based on the information provided in the application forms.
  • Lakshya volunteers visit each shortlisted candidate's home to ascertain the level of need.
  • Based on information from the application forms and from verifiers, our team finalizes a list of scholars based on the organization's financial standing.
